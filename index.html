Cloud Computing Essentials

Cloud computing essentials refer to the fundamental concepts and principles of cloud computing, a technology that enables access to computing resources over the internet. It encompasses understanding key components such as Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS), along with essential concepts like scalability, elasticity, virtualization, and pay-as-you-go pricing models. Mastery of cloud computing essentials involves grasping how cloud services function, their benefits, and their impact on modern IT infrastructure and business operations.
